Danger Zone
  1. Each person writes down Words of Knowledge in the spaces allowed for each category
  2. For groups of 3 or 4
    • Combine the Words of Knowledge from the group to make yout "Treasure Map".
      NOTE: Each member keeps their own list - do not combine lists on a seperate sheet
  3. Choose a beginning location and start looking for the treasure
  4. When you find something on the Treasure Map:
    • Approach the person and say something like...
      • "This may seem a little odd, but we're on a treasure hunt, and we think you're on our list"
    • Show them your list (It may be one or two things from each list, or just one.)
    • Build rapport make friends - ask questions about them to get to know them)
    • Let them know that God has highlighed them, and wants to bless them
    • Ask if you can pray for them
  5. If they say "no"
    • Build some more rapport (common ground - friendsip)
    • Ask the Holy Spirit what he wants to highlight about that person
    • Give them some encouraging words (prophesy)
  6. Ask again if you can pray for them
    • If they say "no" - Bless them and proceed to the next Treasure (person)
    • If they say "yes":
      • Ask for the presence of God to come (release his presence)
      • Command the pain to leave, bones to be set, back to be aligned, tumor to shrink, etc.
      • Ask them to test it out - "Do something that you couldn't do before we prayed"
      • Repeat if necessary
  7. When they are healed:
    • Explain what just happened (the kindness of God revealed - He knows you and cares about you, etc.)
    • Ask them if they would like to know Jesus personally (have a relationship with Him)
    • Have them ask Jesus into their life
  8. Go to the next Divine Appointment on the Treasure Map!
